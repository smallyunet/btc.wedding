Your privacy is absolute. This website runs entirely client-side. We have no databases, no analytics, and no tracking scripts. All inputs, names, and signature coordinates are saved locally in your browser's localStorage. It never leaves your machine.

When you click "Sign & Seal Vows," the page communicates with the public Bitcoin block explorer API (mempool.space) to fetch the latest block height of the Bitcoin network. It writes this block number onto your document as a cryptographic timestamp of when your covenant was sealed.

Once you fill out the details and sign the canvas, you can print the document. Our print stylesheet will automatically format it as a clean, elegant paper certificate without the web buttons. We recommend keeping a physical printed copy inside a fireproof safe, ideally in the same secure location as your cold wallet hardware or seed backups.
